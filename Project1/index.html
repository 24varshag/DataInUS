Quick Guide: What You Can Do

  • Explore Data - View health and socioeconomic disparities across U.S. counties.
  • Select Comparisons - Compare poverty rates with different health conditions using the dropdown.
  • Interactive Scatterplot - Click and drag to select multiple counties and analyze correlations.
  • Choropleth Map - Hover over counties to view detailed statistics on poverty, income, and health.
  • Histograms - Visualize distributions of poverty rates and selected health metrics as they are updated along with scatter plot selections.
  • Live Statistics - Get updates on selected counties' average poverty and health rates.
  • Reset Selection - Use the "Clear Selection" button to reset your analysis.
